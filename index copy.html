How to do the Trail

The Missoula Spirit & Ale Trail provides you with a self-guided tasting tour through our beautiful and friendly mountain town. By following the Trail Map, you’ll visit Missoula’s 9 unique craft breweries and 3 distilleries while tasting an incredible variety of locally made spirits and beer styles. While on the trail, you’ll also get a tour of some of the most scenic spots in town and travel through historic neighborhoods, all while drinking like a local.

Get Started
  • It's easy – either click the "Get Passport" badge to print your own Spirit & Ale Trail passport, or pick up a printed map/passport at any participating Missoula brewery or distillery (known as Trail Stops).
  • Passport in hand, visit each brewery and distillery to taste what they’re making then collect your passport stamp.
  • Taking your time, move along to the next Trail Stop…and the next, and the next… until your passport is full.
  • Mail in your completed passport to redeem your prize! Collect all 9 brewery stamps and receive a custom stainless steel pint glass. Collect all 3 distillery stamps and receive a stainless steel shot glass. Fill your passport and receive BOTH prizes!
Rules
  • You must be 21 and over to complete a passport and claim a prize.
  • This is not a timed contest or competition; there is no deadline to submit a completed passport. This should be fun! Go at a leisurely pace and enjoy what the Trail Stops have to offer.
  • Completing the Spirit & Ale Trail is free – however, each brewery and distillery has their own minimum purchase requirement.
  • Submit one passport per person, per prize redemption. Must submit original stamped passports only; no photocopies accepted.
Redemption (No... not your soul)

So you’ve completed your passport. Congratulations! You deserve a reward.... Or two.

  • To redeem your prize, simply mail in your completed passport to P.O. Box 8411 Missoula, MT 59808.
  • Did you collect all 9 brewery stamps? Then we’ll send you a Custom 16 oz. stainless steel pint glass.
  • Did you collect all 3 distillery stamps? Then we’ll send you a Custom 2.5 oz. stainless steel shot glass.
  • Is your passport completely full? Then, my friend, we will send you both.
  • Both glasses are 18/8 stainless steel, excellent for keeping your beverages cold, and sturdy enough to take with you on your next outdoor adventure.
  • If you want to order more for yourself or for friends who weren’t able to join you on the Missoula Spirit & Ale Trail, we can do that too. Buy Our Nifty Schwag

Tours

The local tour companies can transport you and your group to several of the stops on the Spirit & Ale Trail, safely and in style. Their tours also reach outside the Garden City to breweries throughout the Bitterroot Valley

  • Tap Room Tours
  • Montana Brewery Tours

Bike Rentals

Missoula is an extremely bike-friendly city, with miles of dedicated paths as well as bike lanes throughout the main streets. It really is the best way to see the city. So if the weather allows, get out there and pedal your way along the Spirit & Ale Trail!
